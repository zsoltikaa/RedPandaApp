Meet the Red Panda!

The red panda is a charming, elusive creature that calls the forests of the Himalayas its home. Despite its cuteness, it faces serious threats from habitat loss and poaching.

Why is the Red Panda Special?

The red panda (Ailurus fulgens) is a small, tree-dwelling mammal native to the Eastern Himalayas. Although related to no other bear species, this red-furred animal is a unique and adorable wonder of the wild.
